Ingredients for making Bánh Cuốn

Listed below are the ingredients you will need to make bánh cuốn. Most of these items can be found at your local Asian market, if available, or on Amazon.

Pork Filling

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 teaspoon chicken or mushroom bouillon stock powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (peel and mince
  • 1 small onion (peel and dice)
  • 1/2 cup dried wood ear mushroom (soak in warm water for 30 minutes, drain, rinse thoroughly then mince)

Batter from Scratch

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup potato starch
  • 1/2 cup tapioca starch
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 3 tablesppons vegetable oil
  • 5.5 cups water

Toppings/Garnishes

  • Bean sprouts (steam or blanch
  • Chả Lụa or Vietnamese sausage
  • 4-5 English cucumbers (semi-peel then julienne)
  • Vietnamese herbs
  • Fried shallots
  • Scallion oil
  • Nước Mắm Chấm or Vietnamese fish sauce dipping sauce
